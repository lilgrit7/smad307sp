Color

For as long as I can remember, I've been an artist.

Whether baking, drawing, writing, or making hapzardly nonmusical sounds on pots and pans in hopes of one day playing the drums, I've always loved the act of expressive creation.

Growing up in small-town Virginia and being schooled at home, I was given much creative freedom to explore and express whatever skill (or lack thereof) I chose. I dabbled in creative fiction, poetry, sketch, paint, short film, stop-motion, animation, post-production, video effects, coding, and music theory. Through trial and error, I began picking out what I liked, what I loved, and what where I wanted to go with it.

Graduting Piedmont Virginia Community College in Charlottesville with an Associate Degree in 2012, I transferred to JMU to pursue further study of technical communication, production tech, film and graphical skills, and design theory. As a junior SMAD major, I am slated to graduate in May of 2015 and plan to continue freelance work as a videographer and designer.

Caleb Gritsko - Creative Media - 2014
